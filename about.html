About R2ML

R2 Media Labs (R2ML) is a boutique data science consultancy based in Jersey City, NJ. We operate at the intersection of technology and human health.

Health behaviors are complex. We make up to 200 food and drink decisions per day. Our mental reserves deplete under stress. R2ML believes that by applying rigorous data science to these digital footprints, we can nudge systems and individuals toward better health outcomes.

Leadership

Yll Agimi, PhD, MPH, MS

Principal Data Scientist

Dr. Agimi is a Senior Data Scientist specializing in Public Health and Epidemiology. He received his doctorate from the University of Pittsburgh. With extensive experience in healthcare analytics, he has worked on projects involving the ANAM program, DoD medical readiness, and mental health AI applications. He specializes in bridging the gap between clinical research and Python-based machine learning engineering.
